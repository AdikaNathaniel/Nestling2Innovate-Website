1. Project Overview

The Baiden Etsiakoh Eagle Vision Foundation (BEEVIF) is a Ghana-registered charitable foundation based in Tantra Hills, Accra. Its flagship initiative, Nestling 2Innovate (N2I), delivers STEM, digital skills, and artificial intelligence education to school-age children and out-of-school youth in under-resourced communities.

Operating from the Special Grace Academy (SGA) campus, N2I currently serves 172 learners while extending its reach to neighbouring schools and Senior High School (SHS) graduates. Since 2025, the initiative has successfully run four proof-of-concept programs — Web Design, Global Virtual Internships, Programming, and AI Literacy — and produced one of the first Google Gemini AI Certified (K12) students in Ghana.

N2I is now scaling these pilots into a permanent Community STEM Innovation Centre and a structured digital-and-AI skills pipeline, including expanding access to quality education for rural areas with internet constraints.

Purpose of This Website

The website will serve as the digital home for BEEVIF and N2I — communicating the organisation's mission and story, showcasing programs and impact, and providing clear pathways for learners to apply, volunteers and partners to get involved, and donors to support the Community STEM Innovation Centre and rural access expansion.

Design Direction — Colour Palette

The website will use a cyan blue and white palette, conveying clarity, technology, and openness — fitting for a digital/AI education initiative.

2. Site Navigation — Approved Structure

Following review of comparable organisations (Learn2Earn, 48in48, Global Mentorship Initiative), the navigation has been consolidated into five primary tabs, each using a dropdown menu to organise related sub-pages. Donate and Contact are kept as persistent header actions outside the five tabs for maximum visibility.

1. Home

No dropdown. Mission snapshot, key impact stats, and calls-to-action (Apply, Donate, Volunteer).

2. About
  • Our Story — BEEVIF's origin, N2I's relationship to it, the SGA campus and Tantra Hills setting
  • Our Team — leadership, staff, and key contributors
  • Mission & Vision — organisational purpose and long-term goals
3. Programs
  • Web Design
  • Global Virtual Internships
  • Programming
  • AI Literacy
  • Community STEM Innovation Centre (Coming Soon)
4. Get Involved
  • Apply / Enrol — for school-age children, out-of-school youth, and SHS graduates
  • Volunteer & Mentor
  • Partner With Us — corporates, NGOs, and schools
  • Donate
5. Impact
  • Our Impact — 172 learners served, first Gemini AI Certified K12 student in Ghana
  • Success Stories / Testimonials
  • News & Updates
